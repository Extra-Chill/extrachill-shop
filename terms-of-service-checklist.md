# Terms of Service Checklist

Two documents needed for platform launch: **Site Terms of Service** (all users) and **Artist Platform Agreement** (artists only).

---

## Document 1: Site Terms of Service

Covers general use of all extrachill.com properties for all users.

### Account & Access
- [ ] Eligibility requirements (age, geographic restrictions if any)
- [ ] Account registration requirements
- [ ] Account security responsibilities (password, unauthorized access)
- [ ] Account termination rights (user-initiated and platform-initiated)

### Acceptable Use
- [ ] Prohibited conduct (harassment, spam, illegal activity)
- [ ] Content standards for user-generated content
- [ ] Community forum behavior expectations
- [ ] Consequences for violations (warnings, suspension, ban)

### Intellectual Property
- [ ] Platform ownership of site content, branding, code
- [ ] User license to access and use the platform
- [ ] User-generated content ownership and license grant to platform
- [ ] DMCA/copyright infringement procedure

### Privacy & Data
- [ ] Reference to Privacy Policy
- [ ] Cookie usage disclosure
- [ ] Data collection summary

### Third-Party Services
- [ ] Links to external sites disclaimer
- [ ] Third-party integrations (Stripe, etc.)

### Disclaimers & Liability
- [ ] Service provided "as is"
- [ ] No warranty of availability or accuracy
- [ ] Limitation of liability
- [ ] Indemnification clause

### General Legal
- [ ] Governing law (South Carolina or Texas - confirm with attorney)
- [ ] Dispute resolution (arbitration vs courts)
- [ ] Severability clause
- [ ] Entire agreement clause
- [ ] Modification rights (how ToS updates are communicated)

### Implementation
- [ ] ToS page created on extrachill.com
- [ ] Footer link to ToS on all sites
- [ ] Registration checkbox: "I agree to the Terms of Service"
- [ ] Record of acceptance stored with user account

---

## Document 2: Artist Platform Agreement

Separate agreement artists explicitly accept when creating an artist profile or connecting to Stripe. This is the legally critical document for commerce.

### Eligibility & Onboarding
- [ ] Who can become an artist (age, legal entity requirements)
- [ ] Verification requirements (if any)
- [ ] Stripe Connect account requirement
- [ ] Agreement acceptance mechanism (checkbox during onboarding)

### Artist Profile
- [ ] Accuracy requirements for profile information
- [ ] Prohibited content (hate speech, illegal content, etc.)
- [ ] Platform right to remove/modify content
- [ ] Profile URL and branding guidelines

### Link Pages
- [ ] Acceptable use of link pages
- [ ] External link policies
- [ ] Prohibited link destinations

### Subscriber Data
- [ ] What subscriber data artists can access
- [ ] Permitted use of subscriber emails (artist's own marketing only)
- [ ] Prohibited use (selling lists, spam, sharing with third parties)
- [ ] GDPR/privacy compliance obligations
- [ ] Data retention and deletion requirements

### Marketplace / Shop
- [ ] Platform commission rate disclosure (currently 10%)
- [ ] Commission rate change notification procedure
- [ ] Product listing requirements (accurate descriptions, images, pricing)
- [ ] Prohibited items list:
  - [ ] Counterfeit/bootleg merchandise
  - [ ] Illegal items
  - [ ] Weapons, drugs, regulated items
  - [ ] Items infringing third-party IP
  - [ ] Adult content (if prohibited)
- [ ] Product approval process (if enforced)
- [ ] Inventory accuracy requirements
- [ ] Price gouging / deceptive pricing prohibition

### Shipping & Fulfillment
- [ ] Artist responsibility for shipping
- [ ] Shipping timeline requirements (e.g., ship within X days)
- [ ] Tracking number requirements
- [ ] Shipping label provider (Shippo - artist's own account)
- [ ] International shipping policies
- [ ] Lost/damaged package handling

### Payments & Payouts
- [ ] Stripe Connect terms acknowledgment
- [ ] Payout timing and schedule
- [ ] Currency and conversion
- [ ] Minimum payout thresholds (if any)
- [ ] Holds for disputes/chargebacks
- [ ] Tax responsibility (artist responsible for their own taxes)
- [ ] 1099 reporting disclosure (if applicable)

### Refunds & Disputes
- [ ] Platform refund policy reference
- [ ] Artist obligations for defective/wrong items
- [ ] Chargeback handling procedure
- [ ] Dispute resolution between artist and customer
- [ ] Platform's role in disputes

### Intellectual Property
- [ ] Artist retains ownership of their content
- [ ] License grant to platform (display, promote, etc.)
- [ ] Artist warranty of IP ownership
- [ ] IP infringement reporting procedure
- [ ] Platform right to remove infringing content

### Platform Rights
- [ ] Right to modify platform features
- [ ] Right to suspend/terminate artist accounts
- [ ] Right to remove products or content
- [ ] Right to withhold payouts for violations
- [ ] Right to modify commission rates (with notice)

### Artist Obligations
- [ ] Compliance with all applicable laws
- [ ] Tax compliance
- [ ] Accurate record keeping
- [ ] Responsive customer service
- [ ] Timely shipping

### Liability & Indemnification
- [ ] Platform not liable for artist's products
- [ ] Artist indemnifies platform for product-related claims
- [ ] Limitation of platform liability
- [ ] No guarantee of sales or income

### Term & Termination
- [ ] Agreement effective date
- [ ] Duration (ongoing until terminated)
- [ ] Artist termination rights (how to close account)
- [ ] Platform termination rights (violations, inactivity)
- [ ] Effect of termination (pending orders, payouts, data)
- [ ] Survival clauses (which terms survive termination)

### General Legal
- [ ] Governing law
- [ ] Dispute resolution
- [ ] Severability
- [ ] Entire agreement
- [ ] Amendment procedure
- [ ] Notice requirements

### Implementation
- [ ] Artist Agreement page created
- [ ] Checkbox during artist profile creation
- [ ] Checkbox during Stripe Connect onboarding
- [ ] Record of acceptance stored with artist profile
- [ ] Version tracking for agreement updates
- [ ] Re-acceptance required for material changes
